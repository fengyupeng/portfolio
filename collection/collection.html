Graphic Design Collection

This is a collection of individual graphic design projects I did over the past four years, which covers a variety of medium including branding, typography, book design, photography and package design.

Futura Poster

Typography

This is an individual project done in Fall 2016, advised by Saumya Kharbanda. The goal of this project is to experiment with variables like scale, weight, linespacing, indentation, color, tone, value, texture, and position to design a type specimen poster. Based on my research on the typeface Futura, I created a poster whose visual hierarchy accords with the information hierarchy of the content and whose style is congruent with that of the typeface. Book Cover Redesign

Graphic Design

This is an individual project done in Fall 2016. In this project I engaged form, color and typography to create book covers for a series of books of similar theme. I chose the popular travel guide Lonely Planet as my series and redesigned the covers for three of them. In this project, I experimented with different form and color and engaged the right treatment for type to match with the form to communicate the theme of the book. View full process blog here.
