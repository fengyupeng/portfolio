The Simplicity Book

Photography & Graphic Design

Graphic Design
Fall 2016
Individual Project
Tools: InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator

This is an individual project done in Fall 2016, advised by Saumya Kharbanda. In this project, I made a photography/poetry book around the idea "simplicity". I took around a hundred photos of nude and selected a few to incorporate with Pablo Saborio's poems about Nihilism. The overall theme of this book is to appreciate the simplistic beauty of human body without any unnecessary decoration and to remind us to cherish the simple enjoyments in life.
