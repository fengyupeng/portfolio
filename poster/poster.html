I am a Poster

Even posters are self-awareness

Art & Graphic Design
Spring 2016
Individual Project
Tools: Corel Painter, Illustrator

I AM A POSTER is one of my personal art/graphic design projects. The goal is to raise people's self-awareness and to remind them to always be themselves. Because of the overwhelming popularity of computer science and related disciplines and the overall career-oriented culture at Carnegie Mellon, more and more students switch their major into computer science and related fields; clubs and events related to sports, arts and humanities receive very little attention and support. To remind people to always be themselves and value their own characteristics, personalities, interests, hobbies and aspiration, I designed a series of "self-aware" posters showing off their individuality and personality. I printed out hundreds of these posters and put them everywhere on campus. Immediately, this project received a lot of attention and feedback. As putting posters on places other than poster boards is not allowed at Carnegie Mellon, I left a fake name Christopher Fungunaski on each poster.
